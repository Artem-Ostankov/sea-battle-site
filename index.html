Sea Battle Evolved

A fresh look on a familiar old school Sea Battle that turns it into a deep tactical endeavour.

About the game

This game basically combines mechanics of Sea Battle, Chess and WeGo:
1. intuitive old-school Sea Battle ships
2. WeGo mechanics that let's players plan and exececute actions simultaniously in a defined order. So no one needs to wait
3. while planning your actions you may get a tip about your opponents actions to decide a better counter action.

This is an early development version of the game.

Though you can already play game in a browser or for for better experinece you download game.
